We have partnered with Compassion International to provide solarlamps to 36 homes in Zambarauni

We are looking forward to coming up with more modern way of distributing our products which will lead to partnering with manufactures, distributors and financiers so as to contribute heavily on the green economy.

Solar energy is clean renewable energy which should be adopted by everybody due to its reliability and long term cost effectiveness.Currently innovations have been done which have not only created a wide variety of solar products but also have seen a huge reduction of solar energy costs. The cost of Main grid power provide by the Government has risen tremendously. The traditional source of lighting which was wood fuel also has caused a lot of destruction on our environment causing unpredictable weather patterns hence restrictions being imposed on use of wood fuel by the authorities. Kerosene use which complemented wood fuel has also lead to pollution on our environment and also became a health hazard not forgetting its high cost. Therefore the best option is solar energy which is clean, affordable, available and very effective. Cocatech Enterprises Limited through the use of its wide variety of Cocasolar products our aim is to ensure LIVE GREEN LIVE CLEAN initiative is upheld and much savings have been mobilized and with the trainings we do to our customers we hope that the poverty levels will go down in our rural areas

Pay as you go Project.

We are working on a project which will see rural areas which are not connected to the main grid benefit from use of solar energy. We will be working with both day primary schools and secondary schools to have access to the parents who represents households which are not connected to the main grid. Our plug and play system will be used as the charging point and we will be working with the selected schools to facilitate the charging .The portable solar lamps costs will be recovered over a period of time since we are putting into consideration that people in these areas live below poverty line hence over 45% of their income is consumed by purchase of Kerosene. The repayments together with solar lamp charging fees is going to be designed to be less than cash spent to purchase kerosene monthly. According to UNEP 2010 statistics Ksh 715 is spent monthly to purchase kerosene, this figures have far changed since the price of kerosene has also changed greatly hence consumption has remained the same.

Women in Business Project.

We have realized that many women in small businesses are widows and single mothers who must work hard to put food on the table for their families, most of this micro entrepreneurs are forced to close their businesses at sunset since they don’t have means of lighting, we are looking forward to partnering with other organizations to be able to provide lighting to this special group of entrepreneurs so that they can extend their business hours hence increase their profits.

Apart from the portable systems, we are involved in roof top solar installations (permanent installations) for home owners which guarantee over 90% energy independency. Only 4% of rural Kenya is under national grid according to KNBS census report 2010 hence this number greatly relies on kerosene and wood fuel yet the permanent installation nowadays is very affordable. We are looking forward to sensitizing people in those areas to adopt this source of energy to ensure that we continue having clean and unpolluted environment and also for people to save and hence wealth creation.
